Commenting on the role of tears in the Diary of Ignatius, Fr de Guibert wrote: Although the catholic tradition has always had a high esteem for tears of compunction, and even more so for tears as a mystical gift, there is no saint, as far as I am aware, for whom in practice they have had such importance. 85 Clearly, during the period covered by the Diary Ignatius was receiving exceptional gifts of grace and of prayer; and these notes provide first-hand evidence that he is to be included among the mystics of the Church.
